Frequently Asked Questions

Is datax really free?

Yes, datax is completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscription fees, or premium features. All functionality is available to all users.

Is datax open source?

datax is currently free but not open source. We may consider open sourcing parts of the project in the future.

What are the system requirements?

datax runs on Windows 10+, macOS 10.14+, and modern Linux distributions. You'll need Docker installed on your target hosts to deploy applications.

How many hosts can I manage?

There's no limit on the number of hosts you can manage with datax. The application is designed to scale from single-host setups to large multi-host environments.

Do you offer support?

We provide community support through our documentation and user forums. Enterprise support options may be available in the future.

Can I use datax commercially?

Yes, datax can be used for commercial purposes at no cost. Please review our license terms for specific details.
