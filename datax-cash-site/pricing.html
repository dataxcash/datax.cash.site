Frequently Asked Questions

Is datax really free?

Yes, datax is completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscription fees, or premium features. All functionality is available to all users at no cost.

Is datax open source?

datax is currently free but not open source. We may consider open sourcing parts of the project in the future. The application is distributed as a compiled desktop application.

What are the system requirements?

datax runs on Windows 10+, macOS 10.14+, and modern Linux distributions. You'll need Docker installed on your target hosts to deploy applications. Minimum 4GB RAM and 500MB disk space for the desktop application.

How many hosts can I manage?

There's no limit on the number of hosts you can manage with datax. The application is designed to scale from single-host setups to large multi-host environments. Performance will depend on your hardware and network resources.

Do you offer support?

We provide community support through our documentation and user forums. For enterprise users requiring dedicated support, we offer paid support plans with SLA guarantees. Contact us for more information about enterprise support options.

Can I use datax commercially?

Yes, datax can be used for commercial purposes at no cost. The free license includes commercial use rights. Please review our license terms for specific details about commercial usage.

What makes datax different from other tools?

datax is unique in its combination of multi-host management, Docker app store, and country-specific SCREEN technology. Our DATAX tools for migration, sync, and comparison are specifically designed for enterprise data operations. The desktop-first approach provides a seamless user experience that other web-based tools can't match.
